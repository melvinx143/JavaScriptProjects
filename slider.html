At 182 metres, Statue of Unity is the tallest statue in the world.
The statue faces the Narmada Dam and is located on a river island called Sadhu Bet near Rajpipla on Narmada River.
It is located between the Satpura and the Vindhya mountain ranges near Kevadia town in Gujarat.
Around 129 tonnes of iron implements were obtained from nearly 100 million farmers in 169,000 villages across all states for the statue.
The statue will be able to withstand earthquakes and wind speed up to around 100 km per second.
